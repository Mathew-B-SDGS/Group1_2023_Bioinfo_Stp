Understanding R-Numbers and Gene Panels

The National Genomic Test Directory serves as a cataloge of the various genomic tests availble on the NHS. (in england)

R-Numbers are unique identifiers assigned to individual genomic tests within the directory. These alphanumeric codes help streamline the search process, making it efficient for healthcare professionals and researchers to locate specific tests.

Search for a specific R-number to find the associated gene panel, and Download the availble Bed File
